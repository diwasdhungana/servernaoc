About US

We Team NAO coin have come up with a platform to get involved and interact in this rising environment of cryptocurrency. Cryptocurrency is playing an important role in uplifting the standards of financial sectors. For easy and confidential transactions, cryptocurrency is playing vital role. So by analyzing and researching the future benefits of cryptocurrency, we Team NAO coin came up with an idea of this project "NAO coin". This is our 3rd sem project. Our team contains 5 members who gave contributions in frontend and backend for success of this project. Our members are Diwas Dhungana, Rejin Bhakta Kayastha, Bijen Rayamajhi, Ranjit Sapkota, Utsal Krishna Tamrakar
